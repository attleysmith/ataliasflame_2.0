# Atalia's Flame

Experimental role playing game to "write" a fantasy story. Developed in an iterative way with daily tasks from a backend perspective.

## Glossary

### Gods of Tamanor

#### Atalia
The creator goddess, mother of everything. Her sons are Runid and Hora.

(Teremtő isten, a mindenség anyja. Fiai Runid és Hora.)

#### Runid
The god of light. Son of Atalia, father of Gindon and Geton.

(A fény istene. Atalia fia, Gindon és Geton apja.)

#### Hora
The god of never-dying knowledge. Son of Atalia.

(A halhatatlan tudás istene. Atalia fia.)

#### Geton
The god of death. Son of Runid, brother of Gindon.)

(A halál istene. Runid fia, Gindon bátyja.)

#### Gindon
The god of rejuvenation. Son of Runid, brother of Geton. His wife is Alate, their son is Sifer.

(A megújulás istene. Runid fia, Geton öccse. Az ő felesége Alate, fiuk Sifer.)

#### Alate
The goddess of war. Her husband is Gindon, their son is Sifer. 

(A harc istennője. Az ő férje Gindon, fiuk Sifer.)

#### Sifer
The god of natural elements. Son of Gindon and Alate.

(A természeti elemek istene. Gindon és Alate fia.)
